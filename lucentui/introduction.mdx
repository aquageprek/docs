**LucentUI** is a refined, minimalist theme crafted specifically for [Paymenter](https://paymenter.org). With a focus on clean layout, subtle animations, and glassmorphic design, LucentUI is perfect for modern hosting panels or billing platforms.

Designed by [Zaqua](https://zaqua.zerocloud.id), it brings a polished experience without compromising performance.
