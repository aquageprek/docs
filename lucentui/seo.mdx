## Keywords

- **Setting Name**: `seo_keywords`
- **Type**: Text
- **Default**: `game hosting, minecraft hosting, server hosting`
- **Description**: Add comma-separated keywords relevant to your site.

## Author

- **Setting Name**: `seo_author`
- **Type**: Text
- **Default**: `LucentUI Gaming`
- **Description**: Adds author metadata to your site.

## Open Graph Image

- **Setting Name**: `og_image`
- **Type**: Text (URL)
- **Default**: (empty)
- **Description**: Image preview used when your site is shared on social media. Recommended size: **1200x630px**.

<Note>
These settings directly impact how your site appears on search engines and platforms like Discord, Facebook, and Twitter.
